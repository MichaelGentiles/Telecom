Fiber Based Systems

Fiber-optic cables enable high-speed data transmission by using light signals to carry information.

Description

Fiber optics transmit data as light pulses through thin strands of glass or plastic, providing significantly higher speeds and greater bandwidth than copper-based systems.

Function

Fiber-optic systems are extensively used for high-speed internet, long-distance communications, and as the backbone infrastructure of modern telecommunications networks.
