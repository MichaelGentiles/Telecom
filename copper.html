Copper Based Systems

Conventional telecommunications systems transmit signals through copper wiring.

Description

Copper-based systems, such as twisted pair and coaxial cables, have long been prevalent in telephone networks and DSL broadband. These systems operate by carrying electrical signals through metal wires.

Function

These systems are mainly utilized for voice and data transmission over short to medium distances. While they offer lower speeds compared to fiber optics, they still play a crucial role in legacy infrastructure.
