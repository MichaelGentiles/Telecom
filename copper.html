Copper Based Systems

Traditional telecom systems using copper wiring for signal transmission.

Description

Copper based systems, like twisted pair and coaxial cables, have been widely used in telephone networks and DSL broadband. They work by transmitting electrical signals over metal wires.

Function

These systems are primarily used for voice and data transmission over short to medium distances. Though slower than fiber, they remain important in legacy systems.
